## 基本词义

1. 放弃；权利放弃；弃权

   区别于不容否认（estoppel）

2. 权利放弃证明；弃权证书

## 短语用法

* waiver by election (of remedies)
* waiver of exemption
* waiver of immunity
* waiver of premium clause
* waiver of proof of loss
* waiver of protest
* waiver of right to appeal 
* waiver of tort
